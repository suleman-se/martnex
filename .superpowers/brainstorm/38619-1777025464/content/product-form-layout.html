How should the "Add / Edit Product" form work?

This affects the day-to-day UX for sellers managing their catalogue.

A — Dedicated Page

Navigate to /seller/products/new. Full-page form with sections: General Info, Pricing, Variants, Images, Inventory. Breadcrumb navigation back to the list.

  • Most screen space for complex variant builder
  • Sharable URL (can bookmark mid-edit)
  • Natural for long-form product data entry

B — Slide-over Drawer

Product list stays visible on the left. A wide panel slides in from the right to add/edit. Dismiss to return instantly.

  • Context stays visible (product list in background)
  • Feels lightweight and fast
  • Limited space for complex variant builder

C — Two-Column Page

Dedicated page like Shopify/WooCommerce admins. Left column: title, description, variants. Right column: images, category, status, publish button.

  • Industry-standard pattern — sellers already know it
  • Sidebar for meta: publish, category, tags
  • Best for complex variant management
