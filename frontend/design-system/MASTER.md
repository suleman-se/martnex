# Martnex Design System: MASTER

This document serves as the global Source of Truth for the Martnex project's visual identity and user experience guidelines. It is synthesized using the `ui-ux-pro-max` and `frontend-design` skills.

## 1. Aesthetic Direction: Swiss Modernism 2.0
Martnex adopts a "Precision Silence" aesthetic — a clean, grid-based, rational design system that prioritizes product content and trustworthy SaaS interactions.

- **Keywords**: Grid system, modular, asymmetric, mathematical spacing, rational.
- **Impact Section**: **Exaggerated Minimalism** (Oversized typography and massive white space for hero sections).
- **No-Line Rule**: Structural boundaries must be defined through tonal shifts (`bg-slate-50` vs `bg-white`) rather than 1px solid borders.

## 3. Typography: Geometric Modern
Distinctive yet highly readable pairing that avoids generic system aesthetics.

- **Headings**: `Outfit` (Geometric, contemporary, balanced).
- **Body**: `Work Sans` (Versatile, readable at small sizes).
- **Scale**:
  - `h1`: `clamp(2.5rem, 5vw, 4rem)` (Using Statement sizing).
  - `h2`: `1.875rem` (30px).
  - `Body`: `1rem` (16px, 1.6 line-height).
  - `Label`: `0.875rem` (14px, Medium 500).

## 4. Layout & Spacing Patterns
Based on the **Marketplace / Directory** pattern.

- **Grid**: 12-column modular grid with 1rem (16px) gap.
- **Spacing Scale**: 4pt/8dp rhythm (0.25rem increments).
- **Containers**: Max-width `7xl` (1280px) for desktop; responsive gutters of 1rem (mobile) to 2.5rem (desktop).
- **Elevations**:
  - `Level 0`: Pure background (`slate-50`).
  - `Level 1`: Cards with custom `shadow-premium` (subtle tonal shadow).
  - `Level 2`: Modals/Dialogs with 40% scrim.

## 5. Component Logic

### Buttons (`ui-ux-pro-max` §2)
- **Primary**: Trust Blue bg, white text, `active:scale-95`, 200ms ease.
- **Secondary**: Slate Wash bg, Slate Ink text (No border).
- **CTA**: Vibrant Orange (Used for "Buy Now" or "Become a Seller").
- **Touch Target**: Min 44x44px hitSlop.

### Inputs (`ui-ux-pro-max` §8)
- **Base**: `bg-slate-100` (not white) to merge with card surfaces.
- **Focus**: `ring-2 ring-blue-600/20` + `bg-white` shift.
- **Labels**: Persistent, never placeholder-only.

## 6. UX Hard Truths (Priorities)
1. **Accessibility**: Contrast 4.5:1 minimum; `aria-labels` on all icon-only actions.
2. **Performance**: Server Components by default; `loading="lazy"` on all product images.
3. **Motion**: Exit animations faster than entry (exit 150ms / enter 250ms).
